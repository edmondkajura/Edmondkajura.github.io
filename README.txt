Executive Summary
This project demonstrates a complete Business Intelligence workflow, from raw data preparation to executive-level dashboard reporting. The objective was to analyze operational and business performance for a UAE food delivery company using transactional order data.
Microsoft Excel was used for data extraction, cleaning, validation, and transformation, while Power BI was used for data modeling, KPI development, interactive dashboard creation, and business insights generation.
The analysis evaluated revenue performance, customer activity, delivery efficiency, geographic demand, payment preferences, cuisine trends, and platform performance. The resulting dashboard provides management with actionable insights to support strategic decision-making, operational optimization, and revenue growth.
________________________________________
Project Overview
The UAE food delivery market is highly competitive and data-driven. Businesses require real-time visibility into customer behavior, delivery operations, and financial performance to maintain market competitiveness.
This project was designed to answer key business questions:
•	How is overall business performance trending?
•	Which locations generate the highest demand?
•	Which cuisines drive the most orders?
•	How effective are delivery operations?
•	What payment methods are preferred by customers?
•	Which delivery platforms generate the highest order volume?
Technical Workflow
1. Data Extraction
Raw food delivery transaction data was collected and imported into Microsoft Excel for preprocessing.
2. Data Cleaning & Preparation (Excel)
The following data quality processes were completed:
•	Removed duplicate records
•	Handled missing values
•	Standardized categorical fields
•	Corrected inconsistent formats
•	Validated numerical values
•	Reviewed date integrity
•	Created calculated fields for analysis
3. Data Transformation
Additional transformations included:
•	Revenue calculations
•	Monthly aggregations
•	Area-level segmentation
•	Cuisine categorization
•	KPI metric development
4. Power BI Development
The cleaned dataset was imported into Power BI where:
•	Data relationships were modeled
•	Measures were created using DAX
•	KPI cards were developed
•	Interactive visualizations were built
•	Slicers were implemented for dynamic filtering
•	Executive dashboard layout was designed

Business Analysis & Findings
Revenue Performance
The business generated total revenue of approximately 851.81K during the reporting period.
Key Insight
Revenue remained stable between January and April, indicating consistent customer demand. A noticeable decline was observed in May, suggesting possible seasonal variation, reduced promotional activity, or incomplete reporting data.
Business Impact
Stable revenue performance demonstrates predictable customer purchasing behavior and operational consistency.
________________________________________
Order Trend Analysis
Monthly order volume followed a similar pattern to revenue trends.
Key Findings
•	Strong order activity from January through April.
•	Significant decrease in May.
•	Demand remained relatively stable during peak months.
Business Impact
Consistent order volume supports sustainable revenue generation and effective operational planning.
