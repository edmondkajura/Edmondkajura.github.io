Executive Summary
This project demonstrates a complete Business Intelligence workflow, from raw data preparation to executive-level dashboard reporting. The objective was to analyze operational and business performance for a UAE food delivery company using transactional order data.
Microsoft Excel was used for data extraction, cleaning, validation, and transformation, while Power BI was used for data modeling, KPI development, interactive dashboard creation, and business insights generation.
The analysis evaluated revenue performance, customer activity, delivery efficiency, geographic demand, payment preferences, cuisine trends, and platform performance. The resulting dashboard provides management with actionable insights to support strategic decision-making, operational optimization, and revenue growth.

Project Overview
The UAE food delivery market is highly competitive and data-driven. Businesses require real-time visibility into customer behavior, delivery operations, and financial performance to maintain market competitiveness.
This project was designed to answer key business questions:
•	How is overall business performance trending?
•	Which locations generate the highest demand?
•	Which cuisines drive the most orders?
•	How effective are delivery operations?
•	What payment methods are preferred by customers?
•	Which delivery platforms generate the highest order volume?
